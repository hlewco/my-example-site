Since I was a little girl I have always wanted a big house filled with love and laughter and family. And that it would have a porch, and be made of brick.

Inside the garage there will be shelves for keeping things organized. There will also be a work area/bench for making prodjects.

My dream yard has a big back yard for my children to play in one day. It also has a cozy firepit area with lights strung around it. Lastly, it has a beautiful orchard of fruit trees that will get climbed quite often.
